untitled

                i try to put into words
                what summer means to me.
                warm nights beneath blueish cloud and greenish tree
                you sing to me.
    

    i sit pretty on a tattered bench
    my legs stick, my hands cold and tingly with that springy night chill
    the man with the guitar strums with his fingers and his gentle warbling tongue.
    




                    the little people pass and sing their praises
                    in the form of whistles and claps
                    i bask
                    he plays one last song
                    i sweat out the morning and wave in the eve.
